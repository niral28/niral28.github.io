About Me

I'm Niral Shah, I am currently a Master's in Engineering student at Duke University studying Computer Engineering with a focus in Data Science (Expected Graduation Dec. 2018). Furthermore, I recently graduated Rutgers Univeristy Magna Cum Laude in Electrical & Computer Engineering and Computer Science.

I believe Machine Learning and Computer Vision in tandem with technologies like Augmented Reality are the future! Besides my passion for computer science, I also enjoy tennis, history, photography and traveling!

Over the past few years, I've built strong experience and skills, having recently interned at companies such as Tesla Motors, The Boeing Company, and Verizon Wireless. In addition to working part-time at the Apple Store and as a Teaching Assistant at Rutgers University.
